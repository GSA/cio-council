> The Vision

The Federal Cybersecurity Reskilling Academy offers Federal employees the opportunity for hands-on cybersecurity training, one of the fastest growing fields in the country. This reskilling effort is part of the Administration’s commitment to developing a Federal workforce of the 21st century, as outlined in the President’s Management Agenda and the recent Government Reform Plan.

The inaugural class will be made up of current federal employees who do not work in the IT field, and will give them the necessary training to work as Cyber Defense Analysts within the government.

> The Program

Once accpeted, Academy students will complete a three training course curriculum over three months.

The curriculum begins with a cyber essentials course that includes up to 120 hours of modules, labs, and quizzes. This will include an onsite workshop to launch the course for several days followed by a four-week period of follow-on learning, exercise, and exams. Students will then attend a “bootcamp” course live for six full days, followed by two weeks of study and a certification exam.

Students who successfully pass the certification exam move on to the final course, which follows a similar timeline.

Additionally, Academy participants may complete extracurricular activities and exercises to enhance their learning and skills development, such as online games, cyber competitions, and more. Participants will receive career mentorship and soft skills guidance to help them prepare for re-deploying into the cybersecurity workforce.

> Program Timeline

30 Nov

Apply

The application process will run from November 30th through January 11th.
14 Jan

Assessments

The first assessments will be sent out starting January 11th to the applicants. No prior cybersecurity knowledge is necessary to complete this assessment. The assessment period ends January 25th. Assessments will be emailed. Only applicants that pass the assessment will be considered for the program.
18 Jan

Application Deadline

The program will no longer accept applications after January 18th.
25 Feb

Selection

The program will review, score, interview and select applicants. Applications will be notified of their selection starting February 25th and must complete their registration by the time courses begin on March 11th.
11 Mar

Training

Training will run from March 11th through June 9th, 2019
10 Jun

Graduation
