The Federal Cybersecurity Reskilling Academy offers federal employees an opportunity to advance their careers and develop new skills in the fast-growing field of cybersecurity. The Academy will train two cohorts of 20-25 Federal employees in the skills necessary to work as entry level Incident Response Analysts, Cyber Defense Analysts, Digital Forensic Analysts, or Security Operations Center (SOC) Analysts.




Cohort 1

Powered by: Department of Education, Patriot Strategies LLC & the SANS Institute
  • Open to federal employees who do not currently work in IT
  • Federal employees in the following series are not eligable to apply
    • GS-0854 - Computer Engineers
    • GS-1550 - Computer Scientists
    • GS-0855 - Electronics Engineers
    • GS-2210 - IT Cybersecurity Specialists
  • November through April

Cohort 2

Powered by: General Services Administration, MarCom Group & CompTech
  • Open to all federal employees
  • More details coming in early 2019

Program Timeline

Apply

Applications will be accepted starting November 27th. See above to apply.

Assessments

The first assessments will be sent out starting January 11th to the applicants. The assessment period ends January 25th. Assessments will be emailed. Only applicants that pass the inital email assessment will recieve an interview.

Application Deadline

The program will no longer accept applications after January 18th.
25 Feb

Selection

The program will review, score, interview and select applicants. Applications will be notified of their selection starting February 25th and must complete their registration by the time courses begin on March 11th.

Training will run from March 11th through June 9th, 2019
