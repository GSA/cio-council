The Federal Cybersecurity Reskilling Academies looks to take a cohort of 20 to 25 current federal employees from non-information technology occupations and training them to be entry level Incident Response Analysts, Cyber Defense Analysts, Digital Forensic Analysts, or Security Operations Center (SOC) Analysts.




Cohort 1

Powered by: Department of Education, Patriot Strategies LLC & the SANS Institute
  • Open to non-IT Professions
  • Federal employees in the following series are not eligable to apply
    • GS-0854 - Computer Engineers
    • GS-1550 - Computer Scientists
    • GS-0855 - Electronics Engineers
    • GS-2210 - IT Cybersecurity Specialists
  • November through April



Program Timeline

27 Nov

Apply

The application process will run from November 27th through January 18th.
11 Jan

Assessments

The first assessments will be sent out on January 11th. Assessments will be emailed to participants. Only participants that pass the assessments will recieve an interview.
25 Feb

Selection/Notification

The program will review, score, interview and select applicants. Applications will be notified of their selection starting February 25th and must complete registration for their courses by March 11th.
11 Mar

Training

Training will run from March 11 through June 9, 2019
10 Jun

Graduation
